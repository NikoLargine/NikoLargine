My Role: Solo Developer

As a solo developer, the project was created for the Ubisoft 2024 Game Jam in 14 days, which 9 were spent on the Live2D characters. The project encompasses Live2D characters, Spine2D playable characters, unique weapon for combat (suction cannon), a boss battle, various systems such as an inventory system, dialogue/ VN system, save load, party system, quest system, as well as various unique 2D environments for the player to explore and experience.

  • Gameplay Programming & Systems Design (C#)
  • UI/UX Design & Implementation
  • Spine2D animated characters
  • Live2D animated characters
  • Story and Dialogue
  • Sound Design & Integration
  • Game and Level Design
  • Project Management & Version Control (Git)
